How to Use Clock Kids

  1. Welcome: When you first start, you'll be asked for your name. This helps save your scores and progress.
  2. Main Menu:
    • Learn Time: Practice reading an analog and digital clock. Click "What time is it?" for a new time and "Listen" to hear it spoken.
    • Quiz: Clock to Text: Look at the analog clock and choose the correct time from four text options.
    • Quiz: Audio to Text: Listen to a time being spoken, then choose the correct time from four text options.
    • Quiz: Audio to Clock: Listen to a time being spoken, then choose the correct clock face from four options.
    • View Scores & Progress: See your high scores and a chart of your daily points.
    • How to Use: You are here!
    • About Creators: Learn more about the team behind Clock Kids.
  3. Scoring: You earn 10 points for each correct answer in the quizzes. Your total score and daily progress are tracked.
  4. Sound: Ensure your device's sound is on to hear the time in learning mode and audio quizzes.
